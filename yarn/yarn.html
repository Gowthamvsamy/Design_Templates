Combing of wool

The wool fibres then have to be carded and combed, carding is the process where these entangled random wool fibres and made parallel and brought in straight form and long unit which can be spun in to you, these form of carded wool is called wool tops,which further is combed ( like we comb human hair ) after washing and drying, combing further parallelizes the wool fibres, takes out very short fibres, and also removes residual vegetable matter .
